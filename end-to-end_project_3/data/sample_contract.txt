2. TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 Initial Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of twelve (12) months ("Initial Term").

2.2 Renewal. Following the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods ("Renewal Terms") unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

2.3 Termination for Convenience. Either party may terminate this Agreement upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice to the other party.

2.4 Termination for Cause. Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party: (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of receiving written notice; or (b) becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy.

2.5 Effect of Termination. Upon termination, Client shall pay all fees accrued through the termination date. Provider shall return or destroy all Client data within thirty (30) days of termination.

3.1 Fees. Client agrees to pay Provider the fees set forth in Exhibit B attached hereto.

3.2 Payment Terms. All invoices are due and payable within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month.

3.4 Expenses. Client shall reimburse Provider for all pre-approved travel and out-of-pocket expenses.

5.1 Definition. "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party, including but not limited to business plans, customer data, and technical specifications.

5.2 Obligations. Each party agrees to: (a) maintain the confidentiality of the other party's Confidential Information; (b) not disclose such information to third parties without prior written consent; and (c) use such information only for the purposes of this Agreement.

5.3 Duration. Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for a period of five (5) years.

6.1 Provider Indemnification. Provider shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client from any claims arising from: (a) Provider's breach of this Agreement; or (b) any claim that the Deliverables infringe third-party intellectual property rights.

6.2 Client Indemnification. Client shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Provider from any claims arising from: (a) Client's breach of this Agreement; or (b) Client's use of the Deliverables in violation of applicable law.

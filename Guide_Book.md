# Techverse AI Hackathon Guide Book

## Hackathon Rules
- **Duration**: 48 hours (all work must be completed within this window).
- **Tools & Frameworks**: Pre-built libraries allowed (must be disclosed and used minimally).
- **Code of Conduct**: Professionalism is mandatory.
- **Integrity**: Plagiarism or misuse of AI tools without credit leads to disqualification.

## Judging Criteria
- **Innovation & Creativity**: 15%
- **Technical Execution**: 30%
- **Feasibility & Real-world Impact**: 15%
- **Presentation & Clarity**: 25%
- **Team Collaboration**: 15%

## Approved AI Tools & Frameworks
- **Models**: GPT, Claude, Gemini, HuggingFace models.
- **Vector Databases**: FAISS, Pinecone, Chroma.
- **Frameworks**: LangChain, LlamaIndex.
- **Assistance**: GitHub Copilot.
